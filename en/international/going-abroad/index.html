Going abroad

Home > International > Going abroad

By being a student at the IUT in Lens, you have the possibility of going on international mobility. In addition to the academic or professional aspect, going abroad can be an opportunity to meet new people, discover a new culture or improve in a foreign language. There are two types of mobility, namely study semesters and internships.

Semesters of study abroad

Students of professional license or second year of DUT have the possibility of leaving to study at a foreign university for one semester. The application phase takes place the year before mobility. This must take place in one of the partner universities of the IUT.

Internships abroad

Regarding internships abroad, students can do this in two ways: either in a university laboratory in the form of a study project, or in a company. The laboratory internships take place from the beginning of April to the end of June.

Internships in a university laboratory

In this case, students can complete their internship at a partner university of the IUT. These are not necessarily the same as those that welcome students for a semester of study.

Internships in companies abroad

Internships are an important part of the training of our students. They allow the use of knowledge acquired at the IUT and their application in a professional context.

The subject of the internship is chosen by the company and then validated by the student's home department (IT, Digital Media, Management or Commerce). During the internship, the department monitors the student with exchanges with the company, and as far as possible with visits by teachers to the place of the internship.

At the end of the internship, the student must prepare an internship report and a defense. The internship is evaluated jointly between the company and the department on the following elements:

  • Work performed in a company, with regard to the objectives set in the agreement
  • The written report
  • The defense, evaluated by a jury made up of the training supervisor, the tutor teacher and a permanent teacher from the department.

Your internship can be carried out in a company abroad. It is therefore necessary that you find this internship by yourself.

If you are doing your internship in Europe, you can benefit from Erasmus + scholarships.

FAQ

What conditions must I meet to be able to go on mobility?

If you wish to join a partner university, a selection phase takes place within the IUT. The criteria taken into account are:

  • The results and the seriousness in the work
  • Attendance in progress
  • The behaviour
  • Language level

Certain establishments also set conditions for being able to study there, such as:

  • Academic results
  • Take an English test
  • A limited number of places per establishment
What costs should I expect during my stay?

Besides spending on food or accommodation, you may have to buy school supplies and books. So remember to budget for it. Also ask about the cost of current products.

How to finance my studies or my internship abroad?

There are a number of aids when you go on international mobility. To benefit from it, you must make a request to the International Relations department of the University of Artois. The amounts and conditions are presented at this address.
Note: you must plan a sufficient budget before leaving because the aid is often paid several weeks after your arrival.

I'd like to go abroad but my language level is not sufficient. What to do?

The University of Artois allows students to follow free language reinforcement courses and level tests in language centers. For more information, you can consult this link.
In addition, it is possible to go to a French-speaking country like Canada for example.
